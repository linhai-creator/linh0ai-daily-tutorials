# 1. GOLDEN HOUR ☀️ — 1 giờ trước hoàng hôn
#    Tone: warm, romantic, nostalgic
#    Direction: low angle, side
#    Keyword: "golden hour lighting", "warm sunset glow"

# 2. BLUE HOUR 🌌 — 30 phút sau hoàng hôn / trước bình minh
#    Tone: cool, moody, mysterious
#    Direction: ambient, no direct sun
#    Keyword: "blue hour lighting", "cool dawn ambiance"

# 3. RIM LIGHTING ✨ — ánh sáng viền outline subject
#    Tone: dramatic, editorial
#    Direction: backlight + slight side
#    Keyword: "rim lighting", "edge light outlining subject"

# 4. REMBRANDT 🎭 — half-face shadow với "tam giác sáng" trên má
#    Tone: classical, formal, painterly
#    Direction: 45 degree above + side
#    Keyword: "Rembrandt lighting", "triangle of light on cheek"

# 5. SPLIT LIGHTING ⚡ — chia mặt 50/50 sáng-tối
#    Tone: dramatic, high contrast, mysterious
#    Direction: 90 degree side
#    Keyword: "split lighting", "half face in shadow"

# 6. BACKLIGHT 🔆 — sun behind subject
#    Tone: ethereal, dreamy, romantic
#    Direction: pure backlight
#    Keyword: "backlit", "sun behind subject", "halo effect"
